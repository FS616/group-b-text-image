consideration [kənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃn]
noun
1.1 [uncountable, countable] (formal) the act of thinking carefully about something
1.2 [countable] something that must be thought about when you are planning or deciding something
1.3 [uncountable] the quality of being sensitive towards others and thinking about their wishes and feelings
1.4 [countable] (formal) a reward or payment for a service
